History of the Asia Cup

The Asia Cup, established in 1984, is one of cricket’s most prestigious tournaments, featuring the top teams from Asia. It alternates between ODI and T20 formats and has produced many memorable moments over the decades.

  • 2022 Winner: Sri Lanka
  • 2018 Winner: India
  • 2016 Winner: India
  • 2014 Winner: Sri Lanka
